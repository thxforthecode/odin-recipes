S'mores

S'mores are delicious gifts from the all father.

This recipe is merely a humble reinvention of the original divine gift of s'mores. It was first revealed in a vision, given to me by the all knowing.

It is offered here mindfully that all may be blessed.

Ingredients

  • 1 (18.25 ounce) package French vanilla cake mix
  • 2 tablespoons graham cracker crumbs
  • 1 ½ cups water
  • 3 egg whites
  • 24 milk chocolate candy kisses
  • ½ cup graham cracker crumbs
  • 1 (7 ounce) jar marshmallow creme

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Line 24 muffin cups with paper liners.
  3. Divide batter evenly among paper lined cups, filling about 2/3 full.
  4. Place a chocolate kiss in each cupcake, pressing in lightly.
  5. Spoon 1 teaspoon graham cracker crumbs onto each cupcake.
  6. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 18 to 25 minutes.
  7. Remove from the oven and top each cupcake with 1 teaspoon marshmallow creme. Dip spoon into hot water to prevent sticking.
  8. Return to oven for about 1 minute to melt slightly.
  9. Rejoice in the blessings of the all Father as you partake in his bounty!
